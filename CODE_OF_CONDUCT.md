# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We are committed to providing a respectful, safe, and productive environment for everyone who participates in this project.

## Expected Behavior

- Be respectful and constructive.
- Focus feedback on the code, documentation, or technical decision.
- Assume good intent when discussing issues or pull requests.
- Keep examples and bug reports free of private credentials or customer data.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, insults, discrimination, or personal attacks.
- Publishing private credentials, tokens, customer data, or proprietary marketplace data.
- Spam, trolling, or intentionally disruptive behavior.

## Enforcement

Project maintainers may remove comments, close issues, reject pull requests, or block users who violate this Code of Conduct.

## Reporting

If you see unacceptable behavior, open a private channel with the maintainer when available, or report it through GitHub's abuse reporting tools.
